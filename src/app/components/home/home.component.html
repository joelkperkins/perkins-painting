about us:

Founded in 1969, Perkins Painting and Custom Coatings supplies painting services to the greater San Diego area and beyond. We pride ourselves in offering competitive pricing while maintaining a production oriented schedule with quality and safety being our utmost priorities. We are an employee owned company and always put family first. We welcome you to be a part of the Perkins family!
